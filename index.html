The Vision

A website that transforms based on which musical world visitors are exploring.

Dual Worlds Concept

Your music spans two distinct worlds - the elegance of classical/concert composition and the energy of video game and TTRPG soundtracks. The website will visually transform as visitors navigate between these sections, giving each world its own unique atmosphere while maintaining a cohesive experience.

  • Classical sections: Elegant, sophisticated, warm gold accents on deep navy
  • Game music sections: Retro-inspired, neon colors, pixel accents, synthwave vibes
  • Smooth transitions: The aesthetic morphs seamlessly as visitors switch sections

Goals for Visitors

  • Discover your music - Easy access to listen across all platforms
  • Hire you for projects - Clear path to commission inquiries
  • Find game music packs - Quick access to your itch.io store
  • Learn your story - Your background, philosophy, and achievements

Site Structure

Seven pages covering all aspects of your work.

1 Home

Dramatic hero section with animated name reveal. Features a "dual portal" letting visitors choose between Classical Works or Game Music worlds.

2 About

Your story, background, musical philosophy, and achievements. Prominent display of the NYC Symposium recognition and your "No AI" commitment.

3 Classical Works

Showcase of concert and piano compositions. Each work displayed with title, year, duration, and embedded player. Elegant aesthetic.

4 Game Music

Full aesthetic transformation to retro/pixel style. Features your itch.io packs with preview players, track counts, and direct purchase links.

5 Listen

Aggregated music discovery - all your platforms in one place. Tabbed interface for SoundCloud, Bandcamp, and YouTube content.

6 News

Social feed aggregating your Instagram and YouTube updates. Keeps the site fresh without manual content management.

7 Contact & Commissions

Simple contact form plus a guided commission wizard that walks potential clients through project type, scope, budget, and details.

Visual Design

Two distinct aesthetics that transform between sections.

Classical Aesthetic

Home, About, Classical Works, Listen, Contact

  • Playfair Display serif headings
  • Slow, elegant fade animations
  • Floating particle effects (musical notes)
  • Sophisticated, contemplative mood

GAMING AESTHETIC

Game Music section

  • Pixel font headers + clean body text
  • Subtle CRT scanline effects
  • Neon glow on interactive elements
  • Retro synthwave, energetic mood

Additional Design Features

  • Dark/Light Mode Toggle - Visitors can switch between dark and light themes
  • Smooth Scroll Animations - Elements fade in as you scroll down

Technical Implementation Details

Click to expand

For the curious - here's how we'll build it.

Framework

Vue 3 + Vite

Styling

UnoCSS (Tailwind-like)

Animations

GSAP + ScrollTrigger

Hosting

GitHub Pages (free)

Performance Strategy

  • Lazy-loaded embeds - Music players only load when scrolled into view
  • Optimized images - WebP format with responsive sizing
  • Fast animations - GPU-accelerated, respects reduced-motion preferences
  • Under 300KB JavaScript - Keeps the site snappy on any connection

Embed Sources

  • YouTube - lite-youtube-embed (224x faster than standard)
  • SoundCloud - Native embeds with lazy loading
  • Bandcamp - Native embeds with responsive wrapper
